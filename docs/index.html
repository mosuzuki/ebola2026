Methods and source priority

Source priority for epidemiology

  1. National authorities: DRC Ministry of Health / INRB and Uganda Ministry of Health.
  2. WHO Disease Outbreak News, WHO AFRO, official WHO statements, and WHO R&D Blueprint publications.
  3. ECDC, Africa CDC, CDC, UKHSA and other public health agencies.
  4. Operational partners such as MSF, IFRC, OCHA, Gavi, CEPI, ReliefWeb and humanitarian actors.
  5. Peer-reviewed papers, preprints, clinical-trial registries and reputable science media.

Research/R&D curation rules

Evidence items are classified by topic and review status. Preprints, expert advisory reports and media summaries are retained because they may be critical for R&D situational awareness, but they are explicitly labelled.

Recommended extension: add automated PubMed/Europe PMC/medRxiv queries and require manual approval before merge.
