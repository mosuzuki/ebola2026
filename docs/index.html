Methods and source priority

Source priority for epidemiology

  1. National authorities: DRC Ministry of Health / INRB and Uganda Ministry of Health.
  2. WHO Disease Outbreak News, WHO AFRO, official WHO statements, and WHO R&D Blueprint publications.
  3. ECDC, Africa CDC, CDC, UKHSA and other public health agencies.
  4. Operational partners such as MSF, IFRC, OCHA, Gavi, CEPI, ReliefWeb and humanitarian actors.
  5. Peer-reviewed papers, preprints, clinical-trial registries and reputable science media.

Automated screening scope

The update script includes Europe PMC query hooks to screen epidemiological research separately from product R&D. Candidate records are saved for review before promotion to the curated trackers.

Epi research query focus: Bundibugyo/Ebola/ebolavirus + transmission dynamics, modelling, forecasting, importation, reproduction number, serial interval, CFR, severity and contact tracing.

R&D query focus: vaccines, therapeutics, monoclonal antibodies, antivirals, diagnostics, animal models, assays and clinical trials.
