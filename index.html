Important: Uganda estimates in this prototype are explicitly scenario-based projections. The Uganda projection layer combines DRC-side Flowminder mobility toward border proxy zones with historical IOM DTM Uganda–DRC border FMP destination profiles from Jan–Mar 2020. They are not observed 2026 cross-border movements or Ebola transmission probabilities.
